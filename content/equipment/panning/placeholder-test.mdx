## Why This Pan?

The Garrett Gravity Trap 14-inch is the pan recommended most often by experienced prospectors to beginners, and for good reason — the riffle design does a lot of work that raw technique can't compensate for when you're just starting out.

## Build Quality

Injection-molded black polypropylene. It will outlast the car you drove to the creek in. The color matters: gold shows up bright yellow against a black pan, which is harder to see in a green or tan pan.

## Riffles & Gold Recovery

The riffles run in two directions — longer riffles on the outer ring, shorter ones on the inner slope. This creates two trap zones for different grain sizes. Most beginner pans have one zone. Fine gold (flour gold) benefits most from this design.

## Verdict

At $12, there is no better way to spend your first gold prospecting dollar. Buy two — one for you, one for whoever you bring with you.
